About HMONGGames

HMONGames was develop by a student by the name of Sony Yang. This is a non-profit app that was built for a school project. Nowadays, our Hmong children have strayed away from the culture and language. Sony's hope is to build a gaming platform that will help teach kids, teens, and adults about the hmong culture and language.


The games will allow users to learn in a fun and interactive way. The developer is based in Wisconsin and this is a fun project to get his foot in the door in the industry.
