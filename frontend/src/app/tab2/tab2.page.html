About HMONGames

HMONGames was develop by students of Maryville University. This is a non-profit app that was built for a school project. The team has come to realize that nowadays, our Hmong children and teens have strayed away from the culture and language. The developers aspires to build a gaming platform that will help teach kids, teens, and adults about the hmong culture and language.


The games will allow users to learn in a fun and interactive way. The developers are based in Wisconsin and this is a fun project to get their foot in the door in the industry.
